FUDAN_ANALOG_EDA_MATLAB_SPICE is my public MATLAB-based SPICE-like simulator. It supports DC, transient, AC, pole-zero, and shooting-method analysis.

The repository shows a compact analog-EDA implementation line alongside the digital and physical-design work on this site.
