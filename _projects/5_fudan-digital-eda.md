Fudan_Digital_EDA is my public repository for digital IC CAD and HLS-style implementation work. It covers IR parsing, CFG and DFG construction, scheduling, binding, and RTL or Verilog generation.

It represents algorithmic and systems-level work that complements the physical-design and tooling projects on this site.
