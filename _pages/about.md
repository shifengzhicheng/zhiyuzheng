**Research:** My research focuses on open-source physical design, reproducible EDA systems, and 3D IC implementation.

**Current work:** I am developing [TaiWei](https://github.com/CODA-Team/TaiWei) and [TaiWei-Pin-3D](https://github.com/CODA-Team/TaiWei-Pin-3D) as reproducible physical-design flows for 3D IC research, including OpenROAD-based experimentation and pin-aware implementation. In parallel, I contribute to [ORFS-Research](https://github.com/ieee-ceda-datc/ORFS-Research) and [OpenROAD-Research](https://github.com/ieee-ceda-datc/OpenROAD-Research), building research-oriented flows and benchmark infrastructure around the OpenROAD ecosystem so that physical-design ideas can be compared, reproduced, and extended.

**Previously:** Before starting the Eng.D. program, I worked as a Software R&D Engineer at IC Prophet Microelectronics. I received my B.Eng. in Microelectronics from Fudan University in 2024.
