I am an Eng.D. student at [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/), affiliated with the [College of Integrated Circuits and Micro-Nano Electronics (ICMNE)](https://icmne.fudan.edu.cn/main.htm). I am advised by [Prof. Zhiang Wang (王志昂)](https://ieee-ceda.org/contact/zhiang-wang) and co-advised by [Prof. Keren Zhu (朱可人)](https://krz.engineer/). My research interests lie in ML4EDA and open-source physical design, with a focus on 3D IC implementation and timing closure.

**Current work:** I am developing [TaiWei](https://github.com/CODA-Team/TaiWei) as a platform for 3D IC physical design and [TaiWei-Pin-3D](https://github.com/CODA-Team/TaiWei-Pin-3D) as an evaluation platform for running 3D IC cases with 2D physical-design tools. I contribute to [ORFS-Research](https://github.com/ieee-ceda-datc/ORFS-Research) and [OpenROAD-Research](https://github.com/ieee-ceda-datc/OpenROAD-Research), building research flows and benchmark infrastructure around OpenROAD. I also develop **Agentic OpenROAD** to explore agentic AI for reasoning about and optimizing physical-design workflows.

**Previously:** Before starting the Eng.D. program, I worked as a Software R&D Engineer at IC Prophet Microelectronics. I received my B.Eng. in Microelectronics from Fudan University in 2024.

## Research Interests

- Machine learning for electronic design automation (ML4EDA)
- 3D IC physical design, implementation, and timing closure
- Open-source EDA flows and OpenROAD-based research
- Reproducible physical-design benchmarking and optimization
